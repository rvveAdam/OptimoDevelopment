1. Planning and sketching
I draw a room plan, marking windows, doors, and outlets. I create several layouts, then draw the final version on a new sheet of paper.

2. Measurements and budget
I measure the square footage of the walls and floor to calculate the amount of paint and verify the dimensions of new furniture. I make a checklist and check my basement for supplies to avoid overspending.

3. Preparation and logistics
I remove old furniture and secure the area (floor, windows) with masking tape and foil. I order the chosen furniture and paints online based on my notes.

4. Wall treatment and painting
I fill cavities with joint compound, then sand the walls. After priming, I start painting from the ceiling. After it dries, I apply layers of paint to the walls.

5. Assembly and decoration
I assemble new furniture according to the instructions and arrange it as planned. I remove the foil, reconnect the electronics, and decorate the room with plants and curtains.
